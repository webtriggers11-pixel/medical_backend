Key relationships at a glance

companies 1 → many zones, cities, stores, candidates root of everything
stores 1 → many candidates employees per branch
labs 1 → many lab_bundled_tests → panels lab owns test packages
panels + companies many → many company_panel_pricing custom price per company
candidates 1 → many bookings → reports one candidate, many checkups
bookings 1 → 1 reports one booking, one result
